Perfect Photo Size

Make your photos fit everywhere

But what is it ?

Perfect Photo Size is an online photo editor that allows you to edit your photos to match the sizes expected on social networks such as Facebook & LinkedIn or on applications such as Discord.
The goal is to prevent these websites from degrading your photos by cropping them when they appear in the news feed.

How does it work ?

Perfect Photo Size will transform your image so that its ratio matches the standards of the sites that will host your photo. Unlike modifications such as cropping or resizing, Perfect Photo Size preserves the soul of your image by removing the areas of your image that are the least eye-catching.
